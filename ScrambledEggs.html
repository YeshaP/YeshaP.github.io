Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients Measurements
Eggs 3 Eggs
Salt 3 Teaspoons
Butter 1 Tablespoon
  1. Whisk eggs and salt in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and add butter to it.
  3. Once the butter is melted add in the egg mixture.
  4. Take a spatula to form large soft curds and serve!
